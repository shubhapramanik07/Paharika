Must Visit Locations

Tiger Hill
Scenic View

Famous for its sunrise view over Mt. Kanchenjunga and the Himalayas.

Best visited early morning (around 4 AM).

Batasia Loop & War Memorial
Cultural

A scenic railway loop with a garden and a memorial for Gorkha soldiers.

Offers great views of Darjeeling's toy train.
