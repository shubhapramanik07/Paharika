Guide Terms of Service
1. Eligibility Requirements

To become a certified guide, you must be at least 18 years old, have legal authorization to work in India, and possess adequate knowledge of the areas you wish to guide in.

2. Background Checks

All guides must pass our background verification process. We reserve the right to reject any application that doesn't meet our safety standards.

3. Service Standards

Guides are expected to maintain professional conduct, arrive on time for all bookings, and provide accurate information to travelers. Discrimination of any kind will not be tolerated.

4. Compensation

Guides receive 80% of the tour price, with 20% retained by the platform for marketing, insurance, and administrative costs. Payments are processed weekly via bank transfer.

5. Insurance

While we provide basic accident insurance, guides are responsible for their own health insurance and any additional coverage they may require.

6. Content Rights

By submitting photos or content to your profile, you grant us permission to use them for marketing purposes while maintaining your attribution.

7. Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days notice. We reserve the right to immediately remove guides who violate our terms or receive consistent poor ratings.
